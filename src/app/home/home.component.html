Our Church

Seventh Day Adventist International Church

Bochumer Str. 229, Herne

Pastor: Andy Weber

Seventh-day Adventist International Herne Church is a welcoming congregation of the Adventist Church in the heart of Herne, North Rhine-Westphalia, part of the Ruhr region. Our mission is to build God’s Kingdom by helping people discover Jesus and grow in a faith-filled community. Led by Pastor Andy Weber, Seventh-day International Church offers spiritual guidance, cultural and recreational activities, and meaningful discussions on life’s important topics. Everyone is welcome to join us at LIFELINE HopeCenter and experience the abundant life Jesus promises.

Church Service Program

  • Sabbath Morning Worship

    Our Sabbath begins on Saturday with worship and spiritual fellowship. Sabbath School offers Bible study classes for all ages, fostering thoughtful discussion and reflection on God’s Word. This is followed by the Divine Service, featuring inspiring sermons, uplifting music, Scripture readings, and collective prayer.

    10:00

  • Bible Study and Fellowship

    Join us for Bible Study and Fellowship, a warm and welcoming time to grow in faith, deepen your understanding of God’s Word, and connect with others in our church family. Through engaging Bible lessons, thoughtful discussion, and prayer, we explore the Scriptures together, discovering practical ways to apply God’s truth in our daily lives.

    18:00

  • Afternoon Study and Meetings

    The afternoon provides space for continued spiritual growth. Small group Bible studies and prayer meetings allow members to dive deeper into Scripture and strengthen their personal faith.

    13:00

  • Personal Devotion and Rest

    Sabbath is a sacred time of rest and renewal. Members are encouraged to spend quiet moments in personal Bible study, prayer, and reflection, while also enjoying God’s creation through leisurely walks or family activities. This time allows for spiritual, mental, and physical rejuvenation, helping members enter the new week refreshed and inspired.

    10:00
