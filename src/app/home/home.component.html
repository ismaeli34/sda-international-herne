Our Church

Seventh Day Adventist International Church

Bochumer Str. 229, Herne

Pastor: Andreas Weber

Seventh-day Adventist International Herne Church is a welcoming congregation of the Adventist Church in the heart of Herne, North Rhine-Westphalia, part of the Ruhr region. Our mission is to build God’s Kingdom by helping people discover Jesus and grow in a faith-filled community. Led by Pastor Andreas Weber, Seventh-day International Church offers spiritual guidance, cultural and recreational activities, and meaningful discussions on life’s important topics. Everyone is welcome to join us at LIFELINE HopeCenter and experience the abundant life Jesus promises.

Church Service Program

  • Sabbath School Services

    Our Sabbath School begins on Saturday with a warm welcoming speech and an opening song. We offer engaging Bible study classes for all ages, encouraging discussion, reflection, and a deeper understanding of God’s Word. The service includes a special song, lesson review, announcements, a closing song, and prayer, leaving us spiritually uplifted and inspired.

  • Divine Service

    Join us for Divine Service and Fellowship, a warm and welcoming time to grow in faith, deepen your understanding of God’s Word, and connect with our church family. The service features Scripture reading, inspiring messages, and pastoral prayer, along with gathering of tithes and offerings. Through thoughtful discussion, prayer, and reflection, we explore the Scriptures together, discovering practical ways to apply God’s truth in our daily lives. The service concludes with benediction, sending us forth spiritually refreshed and encouraged.
