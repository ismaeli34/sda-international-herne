Service Teams

Our Service Teams are the hands and feet of Jesus, serving the church and community with love, dedication, and excellence. By joining a Service Team, you can use your gifts to glorify God, support the ministry, and make a real impact in the lives of others. Every act of service, big or small, is a powerful expression of faith in action.

Opportunities to Serve:

  • Hospitality Team: Welcome and assist members and visitors, making everyone feel at home.
  • Media & Technology Team:Support worship, livestreams, and church communications..
  • Ushers & Security Team:Ensure safety, order, and a smooth worship experience.
  • Creative & Arts Team: Use your talents in music, drama, or visual arts to inspire worship
  • Outreach & Community Service Team: Serve the local community through compassion projects and evangelism.

Bible Inspiration:

  • “And let us not grow weary of doing good, for in due season we will reap, if we do not give up.” — Galatians 6:9
  • “As each has received a gift, use it to serve one another, as good stewards of God’s varied grace.” — 1 Peter 4:10
